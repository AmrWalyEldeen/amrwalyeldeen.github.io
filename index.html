About Me

I am an Assistant Lecturer and Researcher in Cell Biology, Histology, and Genetics at Cairo University with extensive experience in molecular oncology, cancer progression, extracellular vesicle biology, and proteomic analysis. My Ph.D. research focuses on proteomic profiling of circulating exosomes from breast cancer patients to identify diagnostic biomarkers for non-invasive liquid biopsy tools.
